TipToe Soles

TipToe Soles is an Australian owned and operated company. We aim to provide comfortable footwear at affordable prices to customers within Australia and around the world.

Our Vision

TipToe Soles have a proven track record in providing high quality products as a white label to various other brands. Our vision is to launch our own brand and establish TipToe Soles as a to-go footwear option, not only in the US but across the world, with plans to expand to Australia and open stores as well. Our first range of products would be comfort or daily wear shoes for you and your family.

Our History

TipToe Soles, is a shoe manufacturing company based out of Texas, USA. We have been manufacturing and providing the products as a white label to various other brands. We now proudly launch our own brand in the market at affordable costs.
